Paris Paloma is a celebrated singer and songwriter hailing from the United Kingdom. She’s best known for her poignant lyrics that beautifully capture the essence of womanhood, delving into the profound realms of female experiences, love, grief, and the mysteries of death.

In her musical tapestry, Paris weaves an intricate poetic narrative, drawing inspiration from a rich tapestry of mythology, art, history, and the romantic spirit. Her songs traverse a wide emotional spectrum, from the tender and heartbreaking to the fiercely assertive and vengeful.
